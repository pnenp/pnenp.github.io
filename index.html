arrow_right Research

I am a PhD student in the School of Computing at the University of Portmouth, UK. I work under the supervision of Janka Chlebíková. My research is currently focused on partition of graphs under constraints, especially for community and community structure detection.

I received a Master's degree in Theoretical Computer Science after graduating from the University of Montpellier in 2016.
